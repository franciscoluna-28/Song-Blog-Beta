ABOUT ME

Hey! My name is Francisco Luna (artistically known as xFran) I'm a computer science student, web developer who creates music as a hobbie. Music was something that I found out of nowhere, I decided to experiment with a program called FL Studio for a while and I ended up loving both music composition and production. I'm currently sharpening my web development skills the most, so I'm able to create more stylish websites and applications.

MY MUSIC

I have to say that my music is a mixture of genres, mostly involving house subgenres (Progressive House, Electro House, Big Room), future genres (Future Bounce, Future Bass) and orchestral music (notice my usage of orchestral elements in most of my tracks, I've always liked to enhance my sound by adding organic elements). Scroll down and you'll see my each track of mine as well as a spotify playlist of them.
